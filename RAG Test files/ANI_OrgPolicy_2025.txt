SECTION 1 — WORKING HOURS AND LEAVE POLICY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Standard Working Hours
- All full-time staff work 45 hours per week across 5 days (Monday to Friday).
- Core hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST.
- Clinical staff in ICU and Emergency units follow 8-hour rotating shifts:
    Morning   : 6:00 AM – 2:00 PM
    Afternoon : 2:00 PM – 10:00 PM
    Night     : 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM

1.2 Leave Entitlements (per calendar year)
- Casual Leave        : 12 days
- Earned Leave        : 18 days (carry-forward max: 30 days)
- Sick Leave          : 10 days (requires medical certificate after 3 consecutive days)
- Maternity Leave     : 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act 1961)
- Paternity Leave     : 5 days
- Emergency Leave     : 3 days (approved by direct manager within 24 hours)
- National Holidays   : 14 days (published in January each year)

1.3 Leave Approval Process
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 7 days in advance via the HRMS portal.
- Emergency leave may be notified by phone/WhatsApp and formalised within 48 hours.
- Leave during December 15 – January 5 requires HOD approval and is subject to 50% staff
  availability.

SECTION 2 — SALARY AND COMPENSATION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Salary Disbursement
- Salaries are credited on the last working day of each month.
- For months with public holidays at month-end, salary is credited one day earlier.
- Salary slips are available on the HRMS portal by the 2nd of the following month.

2.2 Allowances
- House Rent Allowance (HRA) : 40% of Basic Salary (metro cities), 30% (non-metro)
- Travel Allowance            : ₹3,500 per month (staff who commute > 10 km)
- Medical Allowance           : ₹1,250 per month (non-reimbursable, part of CTC)
- Night Shift Allowance       : ₹450 per night shift (clinical staff only)
- On-Call Allowance           : ₹750 per on-call day (specialists only)

2.3 Performance Bonus
- Annual performance bonus is declared in April each year.
- Bonus pool is 15% of the organisation's net operating profit.
- Individual bonus ranges from 0% to 20% of annual CTC based on appraisal rating.
    Rating 5 (Outstanding) : 20% of annual CTC
    Rating 4 (Exceeds)     : 15% of annual CTC
    Rating 3 (Meets)       : 8% of annual CTC
    Rating 2 (Below)       : 0%
    Rating 1 (Critical)    : 0% + PIP (Performance Improvement Plan)

SECTION 3 — IT AND DATA SECURITY POLICY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Password Policy
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters, containing uppercase, lowercase, digit,
  and special character.
- Passwords expire every 90 days.
- Previous 8 passwords cannot be reused.
- After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are locked for 30 minutes.

3.2 Device Policy
- Only ANI-issued devices may access internal clinical systems and patient data.
- Personal devices may access email and the HRMS portal only through the ANI MDM
  (Mobile Device Management) app.
- USB storage devices are prohibited on clinical workstations.
- Screen lock must activate after 5 minutes of inactivity.

3.3 Data Breach Response
- Any suspected breach must be reported to security@anihealthcare.com within 1 hour
  of discovery.
- The IT Security team must notify the CISO (currently Mr. Arjun Mehta) within 2 hours.
- If patient data is involved, the PDPB (Personal Data Protection Board) must be notified
  within 72 hours as per the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.

SECTION 4 — PATIENT CARE PROTOCOLS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Admission Procedure
- All inpatients must have a completed pre-admission checklist signed by the attending
  physician before being assigned a bed.
- Patient identity must be verified using at least 2 identifiers (name + date of birth,
  or name + Aadhaar number).

4.2 Medication Administration
- High-alert medications (insulin, heparin, chemotherapy agents) require double-sign-off
  by two registered nurses before administration.
- All medication errors must be reported within 4 hours using the Incident Reporting
  module in the Clinical Information System (CIS).

4.3 Code Blue Protocol
- Code Blue is declared for cardiopulmonary arrest.
- Response team must reach the bedside within 4 minutes of the alert.
- Resuscitation team comprises: crash team physician, senior RN, respiratory therapist,
  and pharmacist.
- Post-event debriefing is mandatory within 24 hours.

4.4 Discharge Checklist
- Discharge summary must be completed within 24 hours of patient discharge.
- Follow-up appointment must be scheduled before the patient leaves.
- Discharge medications are dispensed by the in-house pharmacy with a mandatory
  counselling session of at least 10 minutes by the clinical pharmacist.

SECTION 5 — PROCUREMENT AND FINANCE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Purchase Approval Matrix
- Up to ₹10,000       : Department Head approval
- ₹10,001 – ₹1,00,000 : HOD + Finance Manager approval
- ₹1,00,001 – ₹5,00,000 : COO approval
- Above ₹5,00,000     : Board approval required; minimum 3 vendor quotes mandatory

5.2 Vendor Empanelment
- New vendors must submit GST registration, PAN card, 2 years of audited financials,
  and 2 reference letters from existing healthcare clients.
- Vendor empanelment review is conducted every 24 months.
- Preferred vendor list is maintained by the Supply Chain team and updated quarterly.

5.3 Travel and Expense Reimbursement
- Domestic air travel (economy only) is permitted for journeys > 600 km.
- Hotel accommodation limit: ₹4,500 per night (Tier-1 cities), ₹3,000 per night (others).
- Per diem meal allowance: ₹750 per day (domestic), ₹2,000 per day (international).
- Expense claims must be submitted within 15 days of travel with original receipts.

SECTION 6 — TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 Mandatory Annual Training
All staff must complete the following every calendar year:
- Fire Safety and Evacuation Drill             : 2 hours (June and December)
- Infection Prevention and Control             : 4 hours (online + assessment)
- Patient Rights and Ethics                    : 2 hours (online)
- Data Privacy (DPDP Act 2023)                 : 1 hour (online + quiz, pass mark 80%)
- CPR/BLS Recertification (clinical staff)     : 4 hours (hands-on, every 2 years)

6.2 Leadership Development Programme (LDP)
- Open to staff rated 4 or 5 in the last two consecutive appraisals.
- Sponsored by ANI Healthcare — covers course fees up to ₹75,000 per year.
- Bond period: 2 years of service after programme completion. Early exit requires
  pro-rated repayment of sponsored fees.

6.3 External Conference Policy
- Staff may attend up to 2 external conferences per year with HOD approval.
- Registration, travel, and accommodation are reimbursed up to ₹25,000 per conference.

SECTION 7 — GRIEVANCE AND DISCIPLINARY POLICY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

7.1 Grievance Redressal
- Grievances must first be raised with the direct manager within 15 days of the incident.
- If unresolved within 7 days, the grievance is escalated to HR.
- HR must acknowledge receipt within 2 business days and resolve within 21 business days.
- Anonymous grievances can be submitted via the ANI Ethics Helpline: 1800-209-7734
  (toll-free, available 24/7).

7.2 Disciplinary Process
- Minor misconduct (e.g., repeated lateness) : Written warning → Final warning → Termination
- Major misconduct (e.g., patient data breach): Show-cause notice → Enquiry committee
  → suspension or termination depending on findings.
- Any termination must be approved by the CHRO (currently Ms. Deepa Subramaniam).
