Product: CardioTrack Remote Monitoring Platform

Overview:
CardioTrack is a remote patient monitoring platform used by ANI Healthcare for cardiac patients.
The platform collects heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, and ECG readings from connected devices.

Key Facts:
- Launched: January 2025
- Active Patients: 12,450
- Monitoring Centers: Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad
- Critical alert threshold: Heart rate above 130 bpm or below 40 bpm
- Average alert response time: 4 minutes
